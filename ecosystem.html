A Circular Reality.

Technology shouldn't end up in a landfill. The Nova Ecosystem is built on the principle of perpetual utility—where every component is reused, recycled, or reimagined.

Open Module Protocol

We’ve opened our hardware schematics. Whether you’re a developer building a specialized medical sensor or a designer creating 3D-printed custom covers, the Nova Dev Portal provides the SDKs and CAD files you need.

Module Recycling

When you upgrade your Core or Camera, your old module isn't waste. Send it back to us for Nova Credits. We refurbish it for the secondary market or harvest its raw materials—Aluminum, Brass, and Gold—for future builds.

The Nova Horizon OS ensures that your data lives independently of your hardware. Switch modules or even swap the entire chassis without ever losing a photo, a setting, or a memory.
