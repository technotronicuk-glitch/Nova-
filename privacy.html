Hardware-Level Privacy.

01 / Data Sovereignty

Nova does not sell, track, or monitor your data. All biometric information is stored locally on your "Compute Core" and never leaves the physical hardware.

02 / Physical Kill-Switches

Because Nova is modular, our Privacy Module allows for physical disconnection of the microphone and camera arrays, providing absolute security that no software can override.

03 / No Pre-installed Trackers

Nova Horizon OS is built without bloatware or telemetry. You own the phone; you own the silence.
